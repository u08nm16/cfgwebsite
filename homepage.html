How to travel sustainably
Image

Some text..

How to get about in a city as a student. Whilst Aberdeen is hardly known for its public transport, there are many different ways to get about easily enough if you’re in the know. In the UK, transportation is the responsible for 27% of Greenhouse gas emissions (data given from the Department for Business, Energy & Industrial Strategy). So when it comes to going green, it’s a very good place to start. There’s a myth among students that you cannot cycle safely in Aberdeen, and whilst there is room for improvement in terms of infrastructure. This is simply not true. Data from a survey collected by students about students showed that approximately 1 in 10 of our fulltime university students cycle, meaning more than a thousand students currently cycle throughout the Granite City.
